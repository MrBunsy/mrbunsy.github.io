Model Mangonel (2005)



The mangonel is a medieval French siege weapon apparently. It's more or less what most people think of when they hear 'catapult', but with a sling attached.

The mangonel is only 20cm tall, but capable of hurling a conker over 10 metres. It's power is easily adjusted by the amount of twists in the string, to provide more or less tension. If I use only two or three turns the range is down to about 3-5 metres, so I can use the mangonel indoors. 4 or 5 turns produces a range of about 10 metres, if I twist it any further the string tends to snap. I've made a trigger out of a piece of wire coat hanger, you can see it being put to good use in my video of the mangonel being fired above
