Shooter

  • Play Larger Version

Shooter is in the style of a 90s flying shooter, written with memories of games like Bomber and Invasion of the Mutant Space Bats of Doom in mind. It is intentionally quite hard to win, and a high scores table will be coming soon!

Controls are WASD to move, enter to shoot and shift to drop bombs. The rest is (hopefully) self explanatory. Look out for boss fights and formations!

Still a work in progress as of September 2012, but it's complete enough to play and be fun!
