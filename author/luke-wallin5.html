Stat Counter 1 Year Old Today!

A year ago today my first hit was recorded in the database for the stat counter. It's come a long way in that time!

From a simple list of browsers for the last week, the counter has slowly morphed into an operating system and browser detection system capable of producing monthly email updates, pie charts, bar charts, average hits per day, and have a fairly good idea of which hits are unique.

Another project which slipped past the one year mark recently is the 2D Engine. I'm on the verge of finishing movable polygons, and I've nearly finished some major updates to the sandbox. Here's what I've managed so far. My next step is some major optimisation and the ability to draw shapes (which combined with movable polygons could be a very powerful tool).
